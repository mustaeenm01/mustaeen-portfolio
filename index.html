Lagrangian Multipliers — Study & Applications

Conducted an in-depth study of the Lagrangian Multiplier method — a powerful technique in constrained optimization from calculus and applied mathematics. Analyzed the theoretical foundations, including the concept of finding extrema of multivariable functions subject to equality constraints.

Developed and delivered a short presentation showcasing real-world applications of Lagrangian Multipliers, including their use in economics (utility maximization), physics (classical mechanics), and machine learning (support vector machines and regularization). The presentation demonstrated the bridge between abstract mathematical theory and practical computational problems.
